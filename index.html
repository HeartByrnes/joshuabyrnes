I'm a content designer/developer with a passion for building elegant, performant applications. My work sits at the intersection of clean engineering and thoughtful design — I care deeply about the details that make a product feel right.

Before going independent, I spent three years at a fintech startup in Melbourne where I led frontend development for a platform used by over 50,000 Australians. I'm comfortable across the entire stack and love working on teams that value craft and collaboration.

When I'm not at my desk you'll find me hiking in the Dandenong Ranges, experimenting with sourdough, or contributing to open-source projects on GitHub.
